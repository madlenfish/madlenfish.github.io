  • Born in Simferopol (Krim / Ukraine), she has got education in Soviet Union.
    Since 1980 she has been working as an art-designer in an advertisement agency in Simferopol. She took part in art design of schools, university and "Aeroflot" Russian International Airlines (ARIA).
  • Since 1997 she lives and works in Delmenhorst, Lower Saxony, Germany.
    In association with Delmenhorst Artists Association participated in art exhibitions in Delmenhorst.
    She placed first at Delmenhorst poster contest in 2006.
    A number of works are lying in private hands.
  • She is specialized in water-color, gouache, pastel, oil and weaving Gobelins.
